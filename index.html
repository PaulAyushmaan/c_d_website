Adaptive LOS Platform

The Adaptive LOS platform integrates Indian OVD validation with a robust Business Rule Engine, enabling efficient and secure loan processing. The system ensures compliance by automating document verification, streamlining approval workflows, and applying dynamic rules to assess eligibility. Additionally, the Smart Admin Portal empowers users with intuitive filtering options, providing real-time insights and facilitating easy management of loan applications. This comprehensive solution enhances operational efficiency while maintaining a high level of security and accuracy.

Holistic Loan Management Platform

The All Rounder LMS platform offers a comprehensive suite of features, including seamless payouts, customizable monthly floating or fixed interest options, and robust support for agreements and legal notices. It also integrates SMS/WhatsApp notifications for timely reminders and updates, ensuring clients stay informed throughout the loan lifecycle. Additionally, the platform includes advanced account finance monitoring tools to track loan performance, providing both administrators and customers with clear insights into outstanding balances, payments, and financial health, ultimately enhancing transparency and efficiency.

Auxiliary Data System

The Auxiliary Data System is designed to enhance verification processes by integrating multiple data sources, including employment, mobile, social media, and bank statement verification. This system streamlines and automates the validation of key personal and financial information, ensuring greater accuracy and reducing the risk of fraud. By leveraging diverse data points, it provides a more comprehensive and reliable assessment, making it a vital tool for businesses seeking to authenticate user details and improve decision-making. The platform supports seamless integration into various workflows, offering flexibility and efficiency across different sectors.
